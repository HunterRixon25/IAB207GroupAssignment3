Miles Davis - Kind of Blue Tour

September 24, 2023 at 21:00

Wheelchair Accessible Alcohol Venue 18+ Identification Required

About this Event

The most influential figure in Jazz is here in Brisbane. Miles Davis takes the stage at The Gresham Bar, performing hits from his classic album "Kind of Blue".

Join us for an unforgettable evening of jazz mastery as we celebrate the timeless genius of Miles Davis. Immerse yourself in the sultry melodies and improvisational brilliance that defined an era and continue to captivate audiences around the world. Whether you're a lifelong jazz enthusiast or new to the genre, this Miles Davis concert promises an evening of musical magic that transcends time. Don't miss your chance to experience the legacy of one of the greatest jazz musicians in history.

Tickets are limited, so secure your spot for this unforgettable night of jazz excellence at The Gresham, Brisbane. Join us in celebrating the genius of Miles Davis and the enduring power of his music.

Location

The Gresham Bar 308 Queen St, Brisbane City QLD 4000
